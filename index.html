Vision

Explora differs from the conventional in terms of its vision. How, you might ask? Over the course of our 6 years of successfully budding potential architects and designers to their path to success, we have evolved our vision in two parts:
Short term vision:
Helping our students attain the right institute to help them shape their careers.
Long term vision:
To monitor and mentor them through the course of their education once they do.

NATA The National Aptitude Test in Architecture (NATA), is an examination conducted for admission to undergraduate Architecture courses at any recognized institute in India. It judges the basic designing aptitude of the applicants. It is mandatory for admission to a recognized undergraduate architecture course anywhere in India.
